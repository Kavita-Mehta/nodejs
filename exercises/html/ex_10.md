### Extra help:
1. Dark green color: #2a4c4c
1. Dark gray color: #e8eaef

1. To have white type you can use the following CSS line
1. To center text search for the **td align** attribute over the MDN site

**Example**
```html
  <table>
    <thead>
      <tr style="color:white;">
        <th>Text in white</th>
      </tr>
    </thead>
  </table>
```
